UniProtKB query definitions (Eukaryota dataset)

Common filters:
- taxonomy_id:2759
- reviewed:true
- fragment:false
- length:[40 TO *]
- (optional) existence:1

--------------------------------------------
POSITIVE SET (Signal Peptide, experimental evidence)
Core logic:
(taxonomy_id:2759) AND (reviewed:true) AND (fragment:false) AND (length:[40 TO *]) AND (existence:1)
AND (ft_signal:*) AND (ft_signal_exp:*)   # experimental evidence (ECO:0000269)

Post-filter:
- keep entries with SIGNAL feature length > 14 aa

--------------------------------------------
NEGATIVE-A (Cytoplasm/Nucleus, no SP)
Core logic:
(taxonomy_id:2759) AND (reviewed:true) AND (fragment:false) AND (length:[40 TO *]) AND (existence:1)
AND (cc_scl_term:"cytoplasm" OR cc_scl_term:"nucleus")
AND NOT (ft_signal:*)

--------------------------------------------
NEGATIVE-B (Transmembrane proteins, hard negatives, no SP)
Core logic:
(taxonomy_id:2759) AND (reviewed:true) AND (fragment:false) AND (length:[40 TO *]) AND (existence:1)
AND (ft_transmem:*)
AND NOT (ft_signal:*)
